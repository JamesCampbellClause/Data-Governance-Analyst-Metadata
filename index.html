The Opportunity

What this role is all about

The Data Governance Analyst, Metadata sits within the metadata pillar of Ageas’s growing Data Governance team, helping deliver and evolve metadata services that enable trusted, discoverable data and AI products across the organisation.

You will work with leading tools including Collibra and Databricks Unity Catalog, developing strategic data capability that directly contributes to business decision-making and data and AI enablement. You will play a hands-on role in supporting delivery, coaching stakeholders, and helping translate data governance solutions into clear business value, all while developing your expertise in a highly visible and evolving team.


This is a rare opportunity to join a growing team during a period of large-scale business transformation and innovation, with strong development and progression on offer. You will have genuine scope to shape how data and AI is governed and valued across Ageas, and to build deep expertise in one of the most in-demand disciplines in the industry.

Your Day-to-Day

Key Responsibilities

🗺️

Roadmap Support

Support the successful planning and delivery of the metadata roadmap, taking on and owning specific deliveries within it.

⚙️

Tooling Development

Develop new capabilities within Collibra and Unity Catalog to enhance the maturity of data governance tools, maximising tooling capability and value for the organisation.

🏛️

Governance Delivery

Work with Data Owners and Stewards to enable data delivery and quality governance outcomes through new and enhanced tooling capability.

📣

Tooling Promotion

Promote metadata tooling value by demonstrating features and capabilities to Data Owners and Data Stewards across the organisation.

🤖

Process Improvement

Identify improvements and process automation capabilities in data processes through the development and application of metadata tooling.

🎓

Stakeholder Coaching

Coach business stakeholders on the importance and value of business context in metadata management, connecting business context to technical metadata processes and platforms.

🔗

Business Context

Bridge the gap between business and technical metadata, helping stakeholders understand how their context feeds into and improves the organisation’s data products.

🌱

Growth & Innovation

Take an active interest in data and AI technological advancements, developing new and automated data governance processes and techniques that complement AI-enabled analytics.

What We Need

Skills & Experience

Skills & Experience You Need

  • Strong understanding of data governance best practice and the purpose and value of metadata management
  • At least two years’ experience in a data governance role within the financial sector
  • Hands-on experience with Collibra or similar tooling such as Informatica, Atacama or Alation
  • Awareness of Databricks Unity Catalog
  • Ability to explain technical concepts in simple, value-led terms for non-technical stakeholders
  • Adaptable, thriving in changing environments and evolving requirements

What Will Set You Apart

  • DAMA qualification, or active pursuit of one
  • Ambitious and eager to build technical expertise in data and AI governance
  • Passion for developing new and automated data governance processes and techniques
  • Active interest in data and AI technological advancements
